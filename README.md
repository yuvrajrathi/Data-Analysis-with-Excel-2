# üìä Supermarket Sales Analysis

This repository contains a Supermarket Sales Dataset used for building analytical dashboards and exploring retail business insights. The data represents real transactional records from a supermarket store, including product details, pricing, taxes, payment methods, profit metrics, and customer ratings.
---

## üìÅ **Dataset Description**

The dataset contains transactional-level supermarket sales records.
Below are the columns used:

| Column Name                 | Description                                                            |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Product line**            | Category of items purchased (e.g., Food, Electronics, Health & Beauty) |
| **Unit price**              | Price of a single unit of the product                                  |
| **Quantity**                | Number of units purchased                                              |
| **Tax 5%**                  | 5% VAT calculated on cost of goods sold                                |
| **Total**                   | Final amount paid by the customer including tax                        |
| **Time**                    | Time of purchase (HH:MM:SS)                                            |
| **Payment**                 | Payment method used (Cash, Credit Card, E-wallet, etc.)                |
| **cogs**                    | Cost of goods sold (Unit price √ó Quantity)                             |
| **gross margin percentage** | Profit margin percentage for each transaction                          |
| **gross income**            | Profit earned from the sale (cogs √ó margin%)                           |
| **Rating**                  | Customer satisfaction rating (1‚Äì10 scale)                              |

## üéØ Project Objectives

The dashboard answers the following key business questions:

1. **Which product line generates the highest total sales?**
2. **Which payment method is most popular among customers?**
3. **What is the relationship between revenue and tax collected?**
4. **Which product categories receive higher customer ratings?**
5. **What is the overall average customer rating?**

## üìà Key Insights

* Certain product lines significantly outperform others in total revenue.
* E-wallet and credit card payments show strong customer preference.
* Tax collected increases proportionally as revenue increases.
* Gross income varies across product categories depending on margin %.
* Customers give an average rating of **~7.0**, indicating overall satisfaction.
